My projects:

  • primitivearc was born as a compiler for a really small subset of Arc targeting the Parrot VM. The original idea was to use that subset to develop a full blown Arc for Parrot, but now the goal of primitivearc is to be a full Arc implementation. In the future, it will also go beyond the language standard.
  • Nyac is a metacircular x86 compiler for a subset of Arc. It runs only under GNU/Linux, 32 bits.
  • LogoWeb is a simple turtle graphics program, written mainly to learn JavaScript and the <canvas> tag.
